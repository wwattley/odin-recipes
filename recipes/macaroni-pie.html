Macaroni Pie Recipe

Description

Macaroni Pie is a beloved Trinidadian and Caribbean dish that combines macaroni pasta, eggs, cheese, and savory seasonings. The pasta is cooked until al dente, then mixed with a custard-like blend of eggs, milk, grated cheese, and flavors like onion and garlic. The mixture is baked in the oven until it forms a golden, crispy top, resulting in a rich and creamy interior. Macaroni Pie is commonly served as a side dish alongside main courses or enjoyed as a comforting standalone meal.

With its irresistible combination of cheese, pasta, and savory flavors, Macaroni Pie is a staple of Trinidadian and Caribbean cuisine. Its warm and indulgent nature makes it a popular choice for gatherings, potlucks, or as a comforting homemade treat. Whether paired with roasted meats, curries, or enjoyed on its own, Macaroni Pie is a beloved dish that embodies the delicious flavors of the region.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni pasta
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cheese (such as cheddar or Parmesan)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings (such as salt, pepper, and herbs)

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, beat the eggs and combine them with milk, grated cheese, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, and desired seasonings such as salt, pepper, and herbs. Mix well.
  3. Preheat the oven to the specified temperature.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked macaroni with the egg and cheese mixture. Stir until the macaroni is evenly coated.
  5. Transfer the macaroni mixture into a greased baking dish, spreading it out evenly.
  6. If desired, sprinkle additional grated cheese on top for a golden crust.
  7. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake until the top is golden brown and the mixture is set, typically around 30-40 minutes.
  8. Once cooked, remove the Macaroni Pie from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
