Macaroni Pie Recipe

Macaroni Pie is a beloved Trinidadian and Caribbean dish that combines macaroni pasta, eggs, cheese, and savory seasonings. The pasta is cooked until al dente, then mixed with a custard-like blend of eggs, milk, grated cheese, and flavors like onion and garlic. The mixture is baked in the oven until it forms a golden, crispy top, resulting in a rich and creamy interior. Macaroni Pie is commonly served as a side dish alongside main courses or enjoyed as a comforting standalone meal.

With its irresistible combination of cheese, pasta, and savory flavors, Macaroni Pie is a staple of Trinidadian and Caribbean cuisine. Its warm and indulgent nature makes it a popular choice for gatherings, potlucks, or as a comforting homemade treat. Whether paired with roasted meats, curries, or enjoyed on its own, Macaroni Pie is a beloved dish that embodies the delicious flavors of the region.
